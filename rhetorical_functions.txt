A. Adding Information 增加資訊:
    For this function, there are two types. 這類功能有兩種類型。
    One is adding information with phrases, such as "in addition", "other", "another" and so on. 一種是用來增加資訊．如"in addition", "other", "another"。
    Another is amplifying with phrases, such as "moreover", "furthermore", "besides" and so on. 另一種是用來加強說明．如"moreover", "furthermore", "besides"。
B. Describing Similarities/Differences 描述相似／相異之處:
    For this function, there are two types. 這類功能有兩種類型。
    One is comparing similarities with phrases, such as "similarity", "similar", "resemble", "correspond", "likewise", "similarly" and so on. 一種是用來比較相似之處，如"similarity", "similar", "resemble", "correspond", "likewise", "similarly"。
    Another is controlling difference with phrases, such as "contrast", "difference", "different", "differ", "on the other hand", "in contrast to", "while", "whereas" and so on. 另一種是用來引出差異之處，如"contrast", "difference", "different", "differ", "on the other hand", "in contrast to", "while", "whereas"。
C. Introducing an Example 提供例子:
    For this function, there are phrases, such as "for example", "such as", "notably" and so on. 此類例如"for example", "such as", "notably"。
D. Expressing Cause/Effect 表達因果關係:
    For this function, there are three types. 這類功能有三種類型。
    First is labeling the reason with phrases, such as "because", "since", "because of", "due to", "factor" and so on. 第一種是用來標明原因，如"because", "since", "because of", "due to", "factor"。
    Second is marking the result with phrases, such as "therefore", "thus", "as a result of", "so that" and so on. 第二種是用來表示結果，如"therefore", "thus", "as a result of", "so that"。
    Last is using verbs to link cause and effect with phrases, such as "cause", "contributes to", "arise from", "derive" and so on. 最後是使用動詞來連接因果關係，如"cause", "contributes to", "arise from", "derive"。
E. Expressing Personal Opinions 表達個人觀點:
    For this function, there are two types. 這類功能有兩種類型。
    One is expressing author's ideas directly and clearly with phrases, such as "in my opinion", "I think", "I believe that" and so on. 一種是直接明確表達作者的觀點，如"in my opinion", "I think", "I believe that"。
    Another is expressing author's meaning indirectly with phrases, such as "It is reasonable to", "It is important that", "Interestingly," and so on. 另一種是間接表達作者的意思，如"It is reasonable to", "It is important that", "Interestingly,"。
F. Expressing Possibility/Certainty POSS 表達可能性/確定性:

    For this function, there are phrases, such as "however", "nevertheless", "nonetheless", "yet", "although", "though", "albeit", "despite", "in spite of", "notwithstanding" and so on. 此類例如"however", "nevertheless", "nonetheless", "yet", "although", "though", "albeit", "despite", "in spite of", "notwithstanding"。
H. Introducing Topics 引入主題:
    For this function, there are three types. 這類功能有三種類型。
    First is introducing main topics with phrases, such as "consider", "discuss", "subject", "issue" and so on. 第一種是用來引入主題，如"consider", "discuss", "subject", "issue"。
    Second is introducing subtopics with phrases, such as "as for", "with regard to", "incidentally" and so on. 第二種是用來引入子主題，如"as for", "with regard to", "incidentally"。
    Last is changing the subject with phrases, such as "another subject", "further question", "turn to" and so on. 最後是用來改變話題，如"another subject", "further question", "turn to"。
I. Listing Items 列舉:
    For this function, there are two types. 這類功能有兩種類型。
    One is introducing the first/second/last item with phrases, such as "first", "second", "last", "begin by", "next", "lastly" and so on. 一種是用來列出第一/第二/最後一個項目，如"first", "second", "last", "begin by", "next", "lastly"。
    Another is briefly mentioning some projects with phrases, such as "etc", "and so on" and so on. 另一種是表示更多例子，與中文的「等等」相似，如"etc", "and so on"。
